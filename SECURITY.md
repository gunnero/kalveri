# Security policy

## Supported scope

Security reports are accepted for the current Kalveri public company website and its tracked static build assets. Private products, infrastructure, DNS, mail, hosting, and third-party services are outside this repository's public support scope.

## Reporting

Please email [contact@kalveri.com](mailto:contact@kalveri.com) with a concise description, affected URL or file, reproduction steps, and potential impact. Do not open a public issue for a suspected vulnerability or include credentials, personal data, private topology, or destructive proof-of-concept material.

Reports will be acknowledged, assessed, and prioritized according to verified impact. A remediation or disclosure timeline will be communicated after triage; no fixed response-time guarantee is implied.

## Public boundaries

Do not publish server names, addresses, accounts, certificate inventories, backup locations, deployment commands, or other operational details. Coordinated disclosure should describe the website-level issue without exposing private infrastructure.
