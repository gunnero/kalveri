# Kalveri screenshot policy

Screenshots in `docs/assets/screenshots/` are recruiter/partner-facing evidence of the public Kalveri company website. They must not include browser chrome, private tabs, admin interfaces, customer names, infrastructure details, unreleased product screens, personal data, or unsupported commercial claims.

Replace screenshots when public positioning or layout changes materially. Do not preserve obsolete screenshots as unnamed duplicates; Git history is the archive.
